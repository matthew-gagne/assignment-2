Gabe Newell

“The easiest way to stop piracy is not by putting antipiracy technology to work. It's by giving those people a service that's better than what they're receiving from the pirates.”
-Gabe Newel

Gabe Newell changed the way gaiming was done on computers by creating "Steam" a digital distrubution service, this service allowes non "tech-savvy" people to download and play games easily.

Timeline

  • He left microsoft to fund Valve in 1996
  • His business partner left in 2000 leaving him as the sole owner of valve
  • During the development of half life 2 he made steam (a game distribution software for PC)
  • As of 2021 he is still the sole owner of valve and has a net worth of over 4 billion dollars
